# Code of Conduct

## Overview

This project is committed to creating the best possible software while sharing our work with the community. We emphasize respect and professionalism in all interactions.

## Guidelines

- **Respect All Individuals**: Treat everyone with courtesy, kindness, and respect.
  
- **Constructive Contributions**: Focus on positive collaboration. Provide constructive feedback and support your peers in their growth and success.

- **Integrity and Professionalism**: Engage in professional communication at all times. Consider the impact of your words, aiming for clarity and respect.

- **Embrace Learning**: Acknowledge that mistakes can happen. Learn from them, and support others in their learning journey as well.
